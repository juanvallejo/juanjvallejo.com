How to play

Click on the dice to roll.

Craps is a dice game based on odds and randomness.

If on your first turn your dice add up to 7 or 11, you win.

View all instructions

If your dice add up to 2, 3, or 12 on your first turn, you lose.

If your dice add up to 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10 on the first throw, that number becomes your point.

To win a round, you must roll the dice until you roll your point.

You lose if you roll a 7 before making your point.

Your score increases for winning a round or rolling doubles.

On losing, your score and round are reset. Your best score is kept.

Your score is based on the double's number and the amount of throws made.
